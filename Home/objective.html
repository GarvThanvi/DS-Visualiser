Data Structure Virtual Lab

            The Objective is to Expose the students to the various key aspects of Data Structures by enabling them to perform
            Linked List and Tree experiments with support of a virtual environment. The primary need for virtualisation here is multifold.


            1.Data Structures is a core course in most of the Computer Engineering Department.

            2.Reinforce theoretical knowledge by providing practical, hands-on experience with data structures.

            3.Provide an interactive and engaging learning environment that encourages exploration and experimentation.
